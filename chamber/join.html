Non-Profit (NP) Membership Benefits

The NP Membership is designed for organizations dedicated to community service and not-for-profit activities. By joining at this level, you will receive:

  • Discounted participation fees for events
  • Access to networking opportunities with local businesses
  • Inclusion in our member directory
  • Priority registration for volunteer and sponsorship opportunities

Make a meaningful impact while connecting with businesses that align with your values!

Bronze Membership Benefits

The Bronze Membership provides small businesses and startups with opportunities for growth and increased visibility. Membership includes:

  • Complimentary listing in our business directory
  • Invitations to monthly networking events
  • Access to valuable business development resources
  • Discounts on workshops and training programs

Ideal for businesses that are just starting out and eager to make their mark.

Silver Membership Benefits

Our Silver Membership is designed for established businesses seeking enhanced support and services. Benefits include:

  • Priority listing in our business directory
  • Access to exclusive networking events and forums
  • 20% discount on chamber-hosted events and sponsorships
  • 20% discount on chamber-hosted events and sponsorships
  • Promotion of your business on our website and social media platforms

This membership is ideal for businesses aiming to expand their reach and strengthen their local presence.

Gold Membership Benefits

The Gold Membership is our premier level, specifically crafted for industry leaders and large organizations. Membership includes:

  • Top-tier listing in our business directory with a featured spotlight
  • Exclusive access to high-level networking events and private runnable discussions
  • 50% discount on chamber-hosted events, sponsorships, and workshops
  • Tailored business promotion plans across our digital and print channels

This membership level is ideal for businesses seeking to take a leadership role in the local commerce community.
